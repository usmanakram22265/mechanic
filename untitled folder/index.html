The Mechanic Behind PitCrew

Marcus Reyes,
15 Years on the Wrench

Born and raised in South Austin, Marcus started fixing neighbors' cars out of his parents' garage at 17. After earning his ASE Master Technician certification and spending a decade at a dealership service center, he realized the model was broken — customers waiting days for simple repairs while life stopped.

In 2019, he launched PitCrew with one truck and a simple promise: show up fast, charge fair, fix it right. Today PitCrew handles 200+ jobs a month across the Austin metro.
